# Doogking - UI Kit & Brand Guidelines

**Generated from visual analysis of brand assets**

## 1. Brand Colors

The brand utilizes a high-contrast, energetic dual-color palette consisting of a deep royal blue and a vibrant golden-yellow, communicating trust, professionalism, and warmth.

| Color Name           | Hex Code (Approx) | RGB             | Usage                                                                                                                 |
| :------------------- | :---------------- | :-------------- | :-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Royal King Blue**  | `#08258B`         | `8, 37, 139`    | Primary brand color. Used in primary logo text ("Doo"), mascot details, typography, and alternating icon backgrounds. |
| **Crown Gold**       | `#FBAE17`         | `251, 174, 23`  | Secondary brand color (Accent). Used in "king" text, crowns, alternating icon backgrounds, and highlights.            |
| **Deep Shadow Blue** | `#051A66`         | `5, 26, 102`    | Used for shading on the mascot (dog's ear) and deep logo accents.                                                     |
| **Pure White**       | `#FFFFFF`         | `255, 255, 255` | Background color and negative-space iconography inside blue circles.                                                  |

_Note: Gradients are subtle, but the golden-yellow elements (like the crown) exhibit a slight linear gradient from a lighter yellow (`#FFC533`) to the base Crown Gold to create volume._

## 2. Typography

The typographic identity is modern, approachable, and highly legible, relying on geometric, rounded sans-serifs.

### Logo Typeface (Primary Brand Font)

- **Font Family Match:** **Poppins** (Black / ExtraBold) or **Nunito** (Black)
- **Characteristics:** Heavy weight, geometric circular 'o's, rounded terminals, friendly but grounded.
- **Styling:** \* Prefix text ("Doo") in Royal King Blue.
  - Suffix text ("king") in Crown Gold.
  - _Custom Graphic Override:_ The second 'o' or the 'g' is replaced/augmented by a stylized dog paw.

### Slogan Typeface (Secondary Brand Font)

- **Font Family Match:** **Montserrat** (Bold)
- **Characteristics:** Clean, modern, sans-serif, wide proportions.
- **Text:** `TODO PARA SU REY, EN UN SOLO LUGAR`
- **Styling:** All-caps. The first half ("TODO PARA SU REY,") is Royal King Blue; the second half ("EN UN SOLO LUGAR") is Crown Gold.

### UI / Body Typeface

- **Font Family Match:** **Proxima Nova** or **Inter** (SemiBold / Medium)
- **Characteristics:** Neutral, highly readable at smaller sizes.
- **Styling:** Used for service descriptions (e.g., "Guardería canina", "Veterinarios"). Sentence case or standard capitalization.

## 3. Iconography & UI Elements

The UI relies on a modular, alternating 5-column layout for services, defined by bold circular badges and distinct typographic markers.

### Badges (The Service Icons)

All icons are housed within perfect circles. The color scheme strictly alternates to create visual rhythm.

1.  **Guardería canina (Dog Daycare)**
    - **Marker:** Large "G" (Royal King Blue)
    - **Background:** Royal King Blue circle
    - **Icon:** Pure White. A house silhouette containing a solid paw print.
2.  **Adiestradores y Educadores (Trainers & Educators)**
    - **Marker:** Large "A" (Crown Gold)
    - **Background:** Crown Gold circle
    - **Icon:** Royal King Blue. A seated dog silhouette looking up at a human hand giving a command/treat.
3.  **Veterinarios (Veterinarians)**
    - **Marker:** Large "V" (Royal King Blue)
    - **Background:** Royal King Blue circle
    - **Icon:** Pure White. A medical cross intersecting with an EKG/heartbeat line.
4.  **Peluquerías caninas (Dog Grooming)**
    - **Marker:** Large "P" (Crown Gold)
    - **Background:** Crown Gold circle
    - **Icon:** Royal King Blue. A dog's profile facing left, flanked by a comb and open scissors.
5.  **Transporte canino (Dog Transport)**
    - **Marker:** Large "T" (Royal King Blue)
    - **Background:** Royal King Blue circle
    - **Icon:** Pure White. A delivery/transport van with a paw print on the side panel.

### Dividers & Structural Elements

- **Vertical Separators:** Thin vertical lines (`1px` width, solid, colored in a muted blue/grey like `#8B9BBC` or `#B0B0B0`) separate each of the 5 service modules.
- **Horizontal Rule (Beneath Logo):** A stylized horizontal divider separating the logo from the slogan.
  - Consists of a central golden heart with a blue paw print cut out.
  - The heart is flanked by a tapering, curved royal blue ribbon/swoosh.
  - Straight golden-yellow lines (`2px` thickness) cap the left and right extremities of the slogan.

## 4. Brand Mascot Details (For Asset Recreation)

If the UI Kit requires the mascot for empty states, loaders, or headers:

- **Subject:** A friendly dog's head looking forward.
- **Color Splitting:** The face is split vertically. The left side is white with blue outlines; the right side is solid Royal King Blue.
- **Accessories:** The dog wears a 3-pointed Crown Gold crown with small circular finials on the tips.
- **Expression:** Happy, open mouth with a small Crown Gold tongue visible.
